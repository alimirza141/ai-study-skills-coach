Each study record should contain:

topic_name
confidence_score
next_revision_date
notes
created_at
Why these fields matter:

topic_name tells us what is being studied
confidence_score gives a rough self-assessment
next_revision_date supports spaced repetition
notes capture context
created_at allows progress over time to be analysed later
